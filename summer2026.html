The Journey

10 Days, Pitch-Backwards Design

We start with the end in mind. Day 1, students record their baseline pitch. Each day builds a specific skill. By Day 10, the transformation is undeniable — captured on video for them to keep forever.

Day 01

Baseline + Foundations

Record first pitch (2 min). Learn what makes ideas stick.

Day 02

Clarity of Thought

Mental models, logic tools, structuring complex ideas simply.

Day 03

Finding Your Idea

What are you passionate about? Idea generation and selection.

Day 04

Building the Argument

Story structure, evidence, logical flow that persuades.

Day 05

Performance Basics

Voice modulation, posture, body language, presence.

Day 06

Theater & Improv

Arts-based exercises for comfort, spontaneity, emotional expression.

Day 07

Draft Pitch + Feedback

Full run-through with peer jury feedback loop.

Day 08

Refine & Deepen

Second-order thinking, tightening the message, cutting fluff.

Day 09

Rehearsal & Polish

Final run-through, individual coaching, tech check.

Day 10

Spotlight Talk

Final 7-minute presentation. Parents invited. Recorded.
